# AtlasUnwritten

## Overview

Atlas Unwritten is a third-person exploration game where players document unexplored environments through photography and scientific research, contributing to a living Atlas that grows with every meaningful discovery.

## Why this project exists

Atlas Unwritten was created to explore a style of gameplay centred on discovery, observation, and scientific documentation.

The original inspiration came from my enjoyment of New Pokémon Snap and the idea of expanding beyond a linear experience into a world driven by exploration and scientific discovery. Atlas Unwritten reimagines that core idea as a free-roaming exploration experience, where players are free to choose their own path and contribute to a living Atlas through exploration, photography, and research. Knowledge, rather than score or combat, drives progression.

## Core Features

### Living Atlas

The heart of the expedition. Discoveries, accepted photographs, observations, and research findings are recorded within a growing Atlas that reflects the team's understanding of the world.

### Exploration and Discovery

Players are encouraged to investigate the environment at their own pace, uncovering new locations, flora, geological features, and other points of interest.

### Scientific Research

Photography serves as the primary tool for documenting discoveries and contributing new information to the Atlas.

### Research-Driven Progression

Progression is driven by discovery rather than combat, experience points, or score systems. New knowledge expands the Atlas and encourages further exploration.
